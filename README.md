# Blind Store Project

## True story

When you are in your desk 👨🏻‍💻, in a meeting and the sun appears behind clouds 🌤 ! Impossible to continue to work 😢 ! Solution ? Control your blinds store over internet to save time and save your eyes 🕶 !
